About Me

Born in Cebu, Phillipines on April 25th, 1995 Loreto moved to America at the age of three. There he was raised in the city of Chicago and studies vocal performance at Illinois Wesleyan University. Soon after graduation he worked as one of IWU's Admission's Counselors. After two years in the position, Loreto moved to NYC where he was hired to be an Executive Assistant at Success Academy High School. Soon after moving to NY Loreto was featured in a plethora of commercials, print ads and dance projects. He studies now to be a full-stack web developer at Columbia University. Feel free to view Loreto's work by clicking on the portfolio tab.
